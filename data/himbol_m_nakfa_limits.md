# Himbol Policy: M-Nakfa Wallet Transaction Tier Limits

**Document Reference: POL-OPS-M01**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Customer Verification Tiers

To comply with local financial services standards, the M-Nakfa mobile money platform operates on a strict three-tiered transaction limit structure based on verified Know Your Customer (KYC) documentation levels. Tier 1 wallets (basic mobile number verification) are capped at a maximum daily transfer volume of 5,000 ERN. Tier 2 wallets (requires submission of national ID card verified at any branch including Asmara Head Office, Keren, or Mendefera) are granted a daily limit of 25,000 ERN. Tier 3 merchant accounts require a verified commercial license and have a daily transaction ceiling of 150,000 ERN.

## 2. Cash-Out Operational Restrictions

Cash-out withdrawals from M-Nakfa wallets can be executed at any brick-and-mortar Himbol branch. Single cash-out requests exceeding 50,000 ERN at rural regional branches (Barentu, Adi Keyih, or Senafe) require a 24-hour advance booking to prevent localized cash liquidity shortages. The Asmara Head Office at Bahty Meskerem Square is exempt from this pre-booking rule and can process single transactions up to 100,000 ERN on demand.
