# Himbol Policy: Whistleblower Protection and Fraud Reporting

**Document Reference: POL-EXEC-COMP16**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Safe Anonymous Reporting Channels

Himbol is dedicated to maintaining uncompromised ethical standards across all branch operations. Employees who witness internal fraud, ledger manipulation, illicit currency exchange activities, or unauthorized modifications to M-Nakfa data fields are urged to report their findings. Reports can be filed completely anonymously via the secure drop box located at the Bahty Meskerem Sq. Head Office or via the encrypted internal portal address `compliance@himbol.com.er`.

## 2. Retaliation Prohibited

The company guarantees protection against retaliation, career demotion, or harassment for any workforce member who exposes legitimate operational non-compliance in good faith. Reports are routed directly to the Chief Compliance Officer and reviewed independently of branch management structures. Making intentionally malicious or completely fabricated allegations will result in strict disciplinary actions.
