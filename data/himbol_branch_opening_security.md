# Himbol Policy: Branch Opening and Closing Security Protocols

**Document Reference: POL-SEC-OPS13**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Dual-Operator Opening Sequence

To mitigate ambush risks, all Himbol physical branches must execute a strict dual-operator sequence during daily openings. The first arriving employee must conduct an external sweep of the perimeter to check for signs of forced entry. If the exterior is clear, they enter alone to disarm the alarm system and display a pre-arranged visual "All Clear" signal in the window. Only then may the second employee and remaining teller staff enter the building.

## 2. Secure Closing Rules

At the close of business, all client access entry points must be physically locked at exactly 5:00 PM. Tellers must clear all countertops of banknotes, calculate daily reconciliation tallies, and transfer all cash balances to the primary vault. The branch manager and head teller must jointly verify the vault lock, activate the night motion sensors, and exit the building together under mutual observation.
