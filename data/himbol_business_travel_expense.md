# Himbol Policy: Corporate Travel and Field Operations Expenditures

**Document Reference: POL-FIN-EXP09**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Inter-City Field Operations Per-Diem Rates

Staff members traveling from the Asmara Head Office to provide logistical support or audit operations at regional branches (Keren, Mendefera, Barentu, Adi Keyih, or Senafe) are eligible for a standard daily meal and incidental allowance of 400 ERN. This per-diem allocation is non-cumulative and cannot be rolled forward. Itemized expense claims with associated receipts are mandatory for all non-per-diem travel expenditures exceeding 100 ERN.

## 2. Approved Transport Modalities

For business travel between cities, staff must utilize authorized corporate pool vehicles whenever available. If an individual must utilize public bus lines, the expenditure is fully reimbursable upon submission of the physical bus ticket stubs. Private vehicle usage for corporate travel requires pre-authorization from the Finance Director and is reimbursed at a flat, documented rate of 4 ERN per kilometer traveled on official routes.
