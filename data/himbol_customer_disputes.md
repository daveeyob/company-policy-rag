# Himbol Policy: Customer Dispute Resolution and Fraud Management

**Document Reference: POL-OPS-DISP12**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. M-Nakfa Erroneous Transfer Claims

If an M-Nakfa user mistakenly transfers digital funds to an incorrect mobile number, they must log a formal dispute claim at a Himbol branch within 48 hours of execution. The Customer Support Team will place a temporary administrative freeze on the disputed funds within the recipient's wallet, provided the balances have not been cashed out. Himbol staff will contact the unintended recipient to coordinate a voluntary reversal; funds cannot be forcibly extracted without a signed customer waiver or legal authorization.

## 2. Remittance Payout Anomalies

In cases where a remittance recipient reports that a payout was wrongfully collected by an unauthorized third party, the branch manager must pull the physical signature log sheet and review the national ID record stored from the transaction. Discrepancies must be compiled into an Investigation File and sent to the Operations Management Team at Bahty Meskerem Sq. within 3 business days for final review and remediation.
