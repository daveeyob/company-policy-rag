# Himbol Policy: Employee Paid Time Off (PTO) and Vacation Guidelines

**Document Reference: POL-HR-PTO08**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Annual Allotments and Accrual Structures

All permanent full-time salaried staff at Himbol receive a baseline benefit of 24 paid time off (PTO) days per calendar year. This leave balance accrues progressively at a rate of exactly 2.0 working days per completed month of active service. Staff members joining mid-year will have their starting allocations prorated relative to their formal employment contract start date.

## 2. Carryover Allowances and Caps

Employees are urged to use their leave balances annually to maintain health and productivity. A maximum of 4 unused PTO days may be officially carried forward into the subsequent calendar year. Any remaining balances exceeding this 4-day threshold are permanently forfeited on December 31st at midnight. Cash payouts for unused leave are not permitted under any circumstances, except upon formal separation from the firm.
