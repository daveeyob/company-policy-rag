# Himbol Policy: Disaster Recovery and Power Outage Continuity

**Document Reference: POL-OPS-BCP11**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Backup Generator and UPS Protocols

To safeguard system availability against localized power disruptions, the Asmara Head Office and all major regional hubs (Keren, Mendefera, and Barentu) are equipped with industrial diesel generators and centralized Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) hardware. In the event of a primary power failure, the automated UPS system will sustain core network equipment for 30 minutes. Tellers must immediately ensure all active transaction windows are saved before the generator sequence triggers.

## 2. Manual Offline Paper Ledgers

If a regional network outage completely severs digital connections to the core server for a period exceeding 2 hours, branches in Adi Keyih and Senafe must pivot to manual offline operational procedures. Tellers must log remittance payouts and cash deposits into physical, bound Paper Vault Ledgers. These manual transaction records must be verified by the Branch Supervisor and synchronized into the digital database within 24 hours of connection restoration.
