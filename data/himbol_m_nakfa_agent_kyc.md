# Himbol Policy: M-Nakfa Retail Agent Registration and Standards

**Document Reference: POL-MKT-M06**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Agent Qualification Criteria

Independent retail operators wishing to register as authorized M-Nakfa Mobile Money Agents must maintain a permanent, physical commercial storefront. Applicants must present a valid national identity card, a clean criminal background certificate issued by local law enforcement within the past 6 months, and an active business tax license. Applications can be submitted at regional branch hubs in Keren, Mendefera, or directly at the Asmara Head Office.

## 2. Branding, Collateral, and Compliance

Every approved M-Nakfa agent must visibly display official Himbol-branded signage containing their unique 6-digit Agent ID number and the official corporate customer care hotline. Agents are strictly forbidden from charging consumers unauthorized convenience fees or processing transactions outside of the official mobile application interface. Violations of these operating guidelines will result in immediate suspension of the agent wallet and forfeiture of accrued commission balances.
