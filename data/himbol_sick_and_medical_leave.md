# Himbol Policy: Employee Sick Leave and Medical Guidelines

**Document Reference: POL-HR-MED15**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Sick Leave Allocations

Full-time permanent staff at Himbol accrue dedicated sick leave at a rate of 1.0 working day per calendar month, capping at a maximum allowance of 12 days per calendar year. Sick leave is designed exclusively to cover personal health issues, emergency medical operations, or the immediate care of a dependent family member. Unused sick leave balances do not carry over into the next calendar year and are reset to zero on January 1st.

## 2. Medical Certification Requirements

Any continuous sick leave absence extending past 3 consecutive business days requires the submission of a formal, signed medical certificate from a licensed health clinic or hospital. The certificate must be uploaded to the HR portal within 5 days of returning to active duty. Failure to provide legitimate medical documentation will cause those absent days to be re-classified as unauthorized, unpaid leave.
