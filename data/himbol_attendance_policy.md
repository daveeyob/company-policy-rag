# Himbol Policy: Workforce Attendance and Shift Verification Rules

**Document Reference: POL-HR-ATT17**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Work Shift Schedule and Clock-In Windows

Tellers, supervisors, and agents at the Asmara Head Office (Bahty Meskerem Sq.) and all domestic branches (Keren, Mendefera, Barentu, Adi Keyih, and Senafe) must clock in by 7:45 AM daily. Operations commence promptly at 8:00 AM. A strict 15-minute grace period applies; arrivals after 8:00 AM are logged as tardy. Accumulating 3 unexcused tardy marks within a single calendar month results in a formal written warning from HR.

## 2. Absences and Emergency Notification Protocols

If an operational resource cannot report for their designated shift due to illness or unforeseen transit events, they must directly call the local Branch Manager by 7:15 AM. Unexcused absences spanning 2 consecutive business days without valid emergency proof trigger automatic disciplinary action, which can include temporary suspension of core network clearance codes.
