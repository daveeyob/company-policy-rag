# Himbol Policy: Remote Account Creation and Ledger Operations

**Document Reference: POL-FIN-BNK03**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Remote Client Verification Framework

Himbol provides specialized banking services including account creation, cash deposits, and withdrawals for remote clients situated outside the immediate vicinity of our primary branch networks. Remote clients must submit a notarized identity application packet accompanied by two colored passport photographs to a traveling Himbol Mobile Agent or via our secure web intake. Accounts remain in a "Provisionally Restricted" state until authenticated by the Central Clearing Unit at the Asmara Head Office.

## 2. Deposit and Withdrawal Ledger Postings

Cash deposits made through regional agents for remote client accounts must be uploaded to the core transaction ledger by 4:30 PM daily. Remote client cash withdrawals are capped at 15,000 ERN per calendar day unless authorized via an overriding supervisor passcode. Regional branches in Keren, Mendefera, and Barentu must run automated reconciliation logs matching daily physical cash balances against remote ledger entries before closing end-of-day balances.
