# Himbol Policy: Corporate Hardware and IT Infrastructure Assets

**Document Reference: POL-IT-AST21**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Assigned Workstation Infrastructure Responsibility

All computing terminals, passport scanners, ultraviolet counterfeit banknote detectors, and thermal receipt printers deployed across teller stations remain the property of Himbol. Staff members are personally accountable for the physical configuration and safety of the terminal units assigned to their operating counter. Workstations must be fully powered down and covered at the close of daily business.

## 2. Field Asset Controls and Safe Return Routines

Mobile Agents operating remote account creation configurations or verifying merchant criteria away from regional branches are issued encrypted mobile tracking devices and secure biometric identity pads. These specialized field assets must be physically logged back into the branch asset rack at the end of each shift. Defective or physically damaged hardware elements must be immediately reported to the central IT Helpdesk located at the Asmara Head Office.
