# Himbol Policy: Corporate Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards

**Document Reference: POL-HR-ETH10**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Professionalism and Operational Transparency

All representatives of Himbol must maintain unparalleled professionalism, absolute transparency, and honesty in every client transaction. Accepting personal gratuities, financial favors, or cash gifts from remittance senders or banking clients is a severe ethical violation. Any employee found accepting tips or bribes will face immediate termination of their contract and potential legal prosecution by state authorities.

## 2. Conflict of Interest Guidelines

Staff members are strictly prohibited from engaging in any secondary external employment or freelance consulting configurations that conflict with Himbol’s core business activities, such as independent currency trading or private lending schemes. Employees must formally disclose any immediate family ties to external retail M-Nakfa agent applicants to ensure completely unbiased application reviews.
