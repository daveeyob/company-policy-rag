# Himbol Policy: Extended Annual Leave and Corporate Work Rotation

**Document Reference: POL-HR-AL19**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Annual Leave Planning Blocks

To support systematic staffing coverage and eliminate transaction clearance delays, employees must coordinate their extended vacation timelines well in advance. Annual leave windows exceeding 5 consecutive business days must be explicitly mapped out and submitted to the division supervisor via the HR system by January 31st for the first half of the year, and by June 30th for the second half.

## 2. Mandatory Uninterrupted Block Allotments

To mitigate internal operational risks and support proactive internal fraud auditing, all personnel handling physical cash, vault entries, or core financial system adjustments must take at least one continuous block of 10 working days of annual leave per calendar year. During this 10-day block, their system access tokens are temporarily suspended, and their operational responsibilities are routed to a rotation officer.
